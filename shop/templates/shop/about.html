About Soggy Potatoes 🌸

The story behind the stickers

Joana 💜

Creator & Artist

Hi there! I'm Joana 👋

Welcome to Soggy Potatoes! I started this little shop because I've always loved creating cute, quirky designs that bring smiles to people's faces.

Every sticker you see here is designed by me with lots of love and a bit of silliness. I believe that the little things in life - like a cute sticker on your laptop or water bottle - can brighten your day!

When I'm not making stickers, you can find me reading Warrior Cats, whale watching 🐋, or cuddling with my cats 🐱

Thank you for supporting small creators and being part of the Soggy Potatoes community! ✨

💜 Our Mission

To spread joy through cute, high-quality stickers and build a community of people who appreciate the little things that make life more colorful.
