What is Stripe? It's how you accept credit card payments. It's free to create an account - they just take a small fee (2.9% + 30 cents) when you make a sale.

To get your Stripe keys:

  1. Go to dashboard.stripe.com/apikeys
  2. Copy your "Publishable key" (starts with pk_)
  3. Copy your "Secret key" (starts with sk_)
